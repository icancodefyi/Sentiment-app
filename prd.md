# SentinelX — AI Communication Risk & Evidence Platform

## 🧠 1. Overview

SentinelX is an AI-powered platform that analyzes digital communication (text, chats, screenshots) to detect sentiment, emotional tone, intent, and potential risk. It goes beyond traditional sentiment analysis by identifying scam patterns and converting findings into structured cases with evidence reports.

---

## 🎯 2. Objective

- Analyze text and screenshots in real-time
- Detect sentiment, emotion, and intent
- Identify scams and risky communication
- Generate structured cases with evidence
- Provide actionable insights and escalation workflows

---

## 👥 3. Target Users

- Individuals (scam detection)
- Customer support teams
- Cybercrime awareness systems
- Academic and research use

---

## 💡 4. Value Proposition

> Convert raw communication into actionable intelligence and structured evidence.

---

## ⚙️ 5. Core Features

---

### 📥 5.1 Input System

Supports:
- Plain text input
- Multi-message chat logs
- Screenshot uploads

Features:
- Drag & drop upload
- Paste input
- Real-time processing

---

### 📸 5.2 OCR Engine

- Extracts text from uploaded images
- Cleans and structures raw OCR output
- Handles noisy inputs (WhatsApp, email screenshots)

Output:
- Parsed text
- Highlighted key entities

---

### 🧠 5.3 Multi-Layer Sentiment Engine

Layers:
- Sentiment → Positive / Negative / Neutral
- Emotion → Fear / Anger / Joy / Sadness / Urgency
- Tone → Aggressive / Polite / Manipulative

---

### 🎯 5.4 Intent Detection

Classifies input into:
- Scam
- Threat
- Complaint
- Normal

---

### ⚠️ 5.5 Risk Scoring Engine

- Score range: 0–100

Categories:
- 0–30 → Safe
- 30–60 → Suspicious
- 60–100 → High Risk

Inputs:
- Keywords
- Emotional signals
- Pattern detection
- Context awareness

---

### 🧠 5.6 Context Awareness

- Analyzes multi-message conversations
- Detects escalation patterns
- Tracks tone shifts over time

---

### 🕵️ 5.7 Scam Pattern Detection

Detects:
- Urgency pressure
- Authority impersonation
- Financial extraction attempts
- Fear-based manipulation

---

### 🔍 5.8 Explainability Engine

Displays:
- Highlighted risky keywords
- Reason behind predictions
- Confidence scores

---

### 📂 5.9 Case Management System

Triggers when risk exceeds threshold.

Each case includes:
- Case ID
- Timestamp
- Evidence (text + screenshot)
- Risk score
- Detected patterns

Statuses:
- Open
- Investigating
- Closed

---

### 📄 5.10 Report Generation

- Generates structured PDF reports

Includes:
- Summary
- Evidence
- Risk breakdown
- Analysis explanation

---

### 📧 5.11 Email Escalation

- Sends reports via email
- Configurable recipients (admin / authorities)

---

### 📊 5.12 Dashboard

Displays:
- Risk meter
- Emotion timeline
- Case statistics
- Trend analysis

---

### 🌐 5.13 Multilingual Support (Optional)

- English
- Hindi
